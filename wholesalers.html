Colombia Brayan Smith - Filter Roast
from $25.00

This coffee was grown by Bryan smith at the farm La Brisas. Brayan is a third-generation coffee grower from Huila who decides to preserve tradition while focusing on improving coffee practices and cultivating new varieties to enhance their specialty coffee crops.

His coffee is harvested following strict ripeness criteria, floated and hand-sorted to remove any defects.

Ethiopia Arsosala - Filter Roast
from $25.00

This beautiful Ethiopian coffee comes to us from the Arsosalawashing station, founded in 2015 that currently serves about 1,200 smallholder producers in the Uraga woreda of Guji. Coffees are picked ripe and de-pulped the same day, then fermented overnight before being washed clean and dried on raised beds. It typically takes the coffee 8 - 15 days to dry under sun and 15 - 20 days to dry when there are cloudy skies.
